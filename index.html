What is parallax scrolling?

Parallax scrolling is a web design technique where background elements move slower than foreground elements, creating a sense of depth and immersion. This effect enhances storytelling and improves visual engagement by simulating a 3D experience as users scroll through a webpage.


Why use parallax effects?

  • Visual storytelling

    Parallax allows designers to guide users through a story-driven experience without requiring multiple pages.

  • Better engagement

    Interactive scrolling keeps users interested longer, reducing bounce rates and improving user experience.


Best practices

  • Use selectively

    Apply parallax effects where they enhance the experience, not everywhere.

  • Optimize assets

    Compress images to maintain fast load times.

  • Depth with color

    Softer background colors and stronger foreground colors enhance realism.
